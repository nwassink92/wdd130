OVERCLOCKED

  • Developer: Magnesium Ninja
  • Genre: Action/Platformer
  • Release Year: 2018
  • Time to Complete: ~4 hours for the main story



In a world run by cybernetic enhancements, a team of vigilantes rises up to fight off a virus that threatens the lives of everyone they know. Take control of Amber and smash your way through walls, robots, and other security measures in a quest to unravel the mysteries behind this virus and who would activate it. The catch: Amber is a glass-cannon and dies in one hit. Making it through each level alive requires skill and precision. Overclocked is a thrilling experience, as navigating each hallway is tense and exciting. Mastering Amber's movement is a treat, especially once you start realizing ways to improve your time completing each mission.

Similar tastes: Celeste, Super Meat Boy

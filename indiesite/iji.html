IJI

  • Developer: Remar Games
  • Genre: Action/Platformer
  • Release Year: 2008
  • Time to Complete: ~4 hours for the main story





When aliens invade her father's workplace, Iji is haphazardly selected by a lab team to become Earth's greatest defender. Now equipped with enhanced strength and other worldly weaponry, Iji must find the resolve to fight off these alien invaders. Is violence the only option? Iji is a game that rewards experimentation and exploration. Using the game's RPG-esque stat system, players are able to find multiple routes through each level, uncovering various secrets and treats along the way. Be wary, for your actions will have consequences.

Similar tastes: Undertale, Cave Story
